Blanket Science & Technology[47] The development of blanket concepts (e.g., solid, liquid, molten salt), materials compatibility studies, thermal hydraulics, tritium transport modeling and integrated testing to validate performance and maintainability. Fusion Plant Engineering & System Integr ation The design and integration of the entire plant system, beyond the fusion engine.
