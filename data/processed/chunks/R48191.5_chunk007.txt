Veterans Health Administration The Department of Veterans Affairs (V A) provides health care and health -related services through the Veterans Health Administration (VHA). VHA ’s primary mission is to provide health care services to eligible veterans. It also provides health care to some family members through the Civilian Health Medical Program of the Department of Veterans Affairs (CHAMPV A).
