Issues for Congress Various concerns related to CZMA have been considered by Congress and have been recently raised by government agencies and various coastal stakeholders. Some of these concerns include the effects of natural and man -made changes on the coast, the effectiveness of CZMA implementation, and CZMA financial assistance authorizations of appropriations and funding levels. These discussions are occurring within the broader context of the 119th Congress and the Trump Administration .
