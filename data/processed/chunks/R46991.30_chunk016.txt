EDA’s distribution and allocation strategy for supplemental appropriations may vary by each event or situation. In recent years, EDA eval uated several factors —including measures of impact and economic distress —to determine supplemental funding allocations for its six regional offices.60 In the FY2025 disaster recovery NOFO, EDA introduced three funding “pathways ” for different types of proj ects.
