Local Technical Assistance Program The Local Technical Assistance (Local TA) program provides grants for management and technical services, including feasibility studies, impact analyses, disaster resiliency plans, and project planning. Analysis from the impact and feasibility studies may help leaders in economic development decisionmaking. For instance, an eligible en tity could apply for a Local TA grant to prepare for a business development project such as an incubator, shared -use processing facility, or an entrepreneurship center.
