These effects can include impacts to servicemember health and deployability , cognitive functioning, and workplace safety . Extended absences or unplanned attriti on can impact a unit’s mission by creating staffing and capability gaps, disrupt ing unit cohesion, reduc ing morale, and perpetuat ing mental health stigma.
