Reinstatement of the FCC’s Spectrum Auction Authority in the 119th Congress P.L. 119 -21 was signed into law on July 4, 2025 . Title IV , Section 40002 , contains several spectrum provisions.34 Section 40002(b)(1) reinstate s the FCC’s general auction authority through September 30, 2034 , except for the 3.1-3.45 GHz and 7.4 -8.4 GHz bands , for which the authority does not apply. In addition, Section 40002 mandates the following actions: • The FCC is required to grant licenses through competitive bidding (i.e., auctions) for 300 megahertz of spectrum, including not less than 100 megahertz in the 3.98-4.2 GHz band within two years of enactment. • NTIA, in consultation with the FCC, is directed to identify 500 megahertz of spectrum in the 1.3 -10.5 GHz range (excluding the 3.1 -3.45 GHz and the 7.4 -8.4 GHz bands) for reallocation from federal use to nonfederal use , shared federal and nonfederal use , or a combination thereof, for full -power , commercial licensed use.
