FSS: escrow -only pilot pro gram §404 §404/§5404 The provisions are substantively the same, though there are drafting differences and two policy differences. The Housing for the 21st Century Act would require that the pilot be established within a year; there is no timeframe specified in the ROAD to Housing Act . The ROAD to Housing Act would authorize appropriations of such sums as necessary to carry out the pilot; the Housing for the 21st Century Act includes no authorization of approp riations. The engrossed version of the Housing for the 21st Century Act has a pilot term of 7 years; the ROAD to Housing Act has a pilot term of 10 years.
