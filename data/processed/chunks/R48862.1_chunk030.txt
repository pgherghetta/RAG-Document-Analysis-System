Other Bands Under Consideration for Reallocation and Auction To meet requirements in P.L. 119 -21, the FCC and NTIA may consider other bands for reallocation and auction. Some bands have been named in previous legislation. Other bands have been under study by agencies or industry for potential for reallocation. Some bands have been identified in the NSS. Others are under consideration globally for future mobile use.
