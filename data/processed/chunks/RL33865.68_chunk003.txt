These include enhancin g U.S. security at home and abroad , promoting U.S. economic prosperity , and promoting free markets and democracy around the world. The United States has used unilateral and multilateral mechanisms to achieve these objectives, with varying amounts of emphas is at different times. These mechanisms have included a range of military, diplomatic, and economic tools.
