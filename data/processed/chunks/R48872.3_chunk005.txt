T ypes of Steelmaking Steelmaking can be divided into two broad categories: primary and secondary. Primary steelmaking produces raw steel from new iron. Iron is produced from iron pellets either at a blast furnace (which uses metallurgical coke) or at a direct reduced iron furnace (which uses methane gas or hydrogen).
