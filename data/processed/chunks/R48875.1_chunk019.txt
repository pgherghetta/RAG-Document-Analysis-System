Defense Policy In 2023, the Albanese government released a Defence Strategic Review (DSR), which examined and offered findings on Australia’s defense spending, capacity, and posture.107 The DSR was prompted by a growing concern amongst Australian security analysts and specialists about Australia’s strate gic environment, including threats to its interests emanating from regional instability due to U.S.-China competition, China’s military modernization, and reduced warning time to prepare for conflict given modern power -projection capabilities.
