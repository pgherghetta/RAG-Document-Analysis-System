Resources and Contacts for Finding Health Care Providers and Facilities Constituents may seek help finding local health care facilities or providers. In addition to the sources below , which provide initial assistance , a number of states offer tools that may help congressional offices respond to such requests. Constituents may also contact their health insurance (e.g., private health insurance, Medicare, Medicaid) to identify providers and determine if provider s are in-network or accept their insurance.
