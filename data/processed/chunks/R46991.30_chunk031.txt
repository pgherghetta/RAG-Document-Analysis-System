EDRA directed OTED to assist tribal communities with accessing economic development assistance, maintain an active role in coordination with other federal agencies, and participat e in rulemaking and consultations that may impact tribal communities. EDRA also required EDA to publish a comprehensive online directory of economic development programs for tribal communities and maintain sufficient staff to carry out OTED outreach activi ties. EDA is to complete a strategic plan for tribal economic development, to be informed by a tribal consultation process, by January 4, 2026 (and every three years thereafter).
