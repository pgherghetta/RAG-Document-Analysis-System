Other concept s are also being researched to deliver the pulsed power needed for inertial confinement fusion, such as electrical currents.23 Federal Programs for Fusion R esearch and Development (R&D) Public investment in fusion R&D can take different forms, including basic plasma physics research, engineering concepts underlying different types of fusion reactors, and weapons -related activities.
