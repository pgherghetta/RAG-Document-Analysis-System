This report discusses only the compensation elements that make up regular military compensation (RMC) . RMC is a statutorily defined measure of the major compensation element s that every servicemember receives periodically , and excludes non -cash benefits and deferred compensation .19 Certain GFOs receive a “personal money allowance” as well , which is not part of RMC.
